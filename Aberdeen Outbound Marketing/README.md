# Aberdeen Outbound Marketing

This folder contains outbound marketing collateral produced by Aberdeen Group between 1996 and ~2001. Unlike the Kastner-authored research studies in `kastner-author/`, these artifacts are **marketing assets**: sales decks, retainer sales kits, and marketing web pages used to sell Aberdeen services to IT vendor and private equity clients.

## Studies in this folder

| study_id | title | year |
|---|---|---|
| `aberdeen-dors-buying-guide-1996-web-5ee808` | DORS 1996 Buying Guide (Wayback capture) | 1996 |
| `aberdeen-aas-retainer-sales-doc-f40a25` | Advisory Access Service (AAS) Retainer Sales Document | ~2001 |
| `aberdeen-corporate-background-deck-f70fe9` | Aberdeen Corporate Background Presentation | ~2001 |

Each subfolder is a complete Frictionless Data Package with `data/`, `schema/`, `source/`, and `scripts/` plus a `README.md`. License: CC-BY-4.0.
