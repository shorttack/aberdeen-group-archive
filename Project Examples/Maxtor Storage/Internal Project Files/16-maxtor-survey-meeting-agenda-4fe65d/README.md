## Abstract

An internal Aberdeen Group meeting agenda for the Maxtor Pools of Storage Enterprise Edition (PoSEE) project Phase 3 — Message Testing. The document outlines plans to conduct 100–250 internet surveys and 5–8 interviews to test market receptivity to the Pools of Storage messaging, with intent to merge results with Aberdeen's April 2003 midline storage survey data. Sample survey questions reveal the research instruments aimed at gauging enterprise storage buyers' awareness, pain points, and openness to the midline storage value proposition.
