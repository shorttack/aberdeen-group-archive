## Original Document Text

Maxtor PoSEE
Survey Organizational Meeting
12/10/03

Participants	Peter Kastner, Dave Hill, John Boyne, Beth Conant

Proposal Description	Phase Three: Message Testing
    - Develop survey instrument
    - Recruit survey population and face-to-face schedule
    - Conduct 100-250 Internet surveys and 5-8 face-to-face or telephone interviews
    - Analyze results versus proposed Pools of Storage messages
    - Analyze Pools of Storage buying intentions
    - Where appropriate, merge this survey with the April 2003 Aberdeen survey results on midline storage
    - Prepare a report on market survey intelligence and Aberdeen's recommend approach to the market
    - Conduct a telephone workshop with Maxtor to re-align proposed messages with market intelligence
    - Collateral: Prepare a short white paper for use as a Pools of Storage talking piece with the press and as the first evangelization collateral.

Meeting Agenda	    1. Confirm internal lead for survey
    2. Confirm format of survey (internet/phone) 
    3. Assemble targets
    4. Plan development and execution of survey (timeline)
    5. Discuss extension of survey dates and potential project impacts if proven necessary
    6. Peter/Brian to identify internal resources to develop/conduct survey

Sample Survey Questions	Is storing data getting easier or more complicated?
What applications do you currently use?
What are your current storage options?
How do you manage your data storage?
How much additional storage capacity did you purchase in 2003?
Does your storage reseller provide a complete solution?
What do you believe will be the next big thing in storage management?
Have you ever purchased the wrong type of storage for your needs?
Do you work with multiple storage vendors or are you locked in to one vendor?
How educated do you feel regarding storage options?
