## Audit-tool hardening

A Layer A bug was also fixed: the original audit script only checked `codes.csv.code_id` for observation_type lookups, which produced 1267 false positives across 54 alt-schema studies where the column is named `code`, `code_value`, `code_label`, or `label` instead. The v2 audit now collects IDs from any of those columns.

## Residual 30 failures (all Layer B, 10 studies)

These are **not data defects** — they are 10 legacy studies that were ingested with non-v18 column layouts before the canonical schema was finalized. They maintain internal referential integrity (Layer A = 0) and proper QUOTE_ALL formatting; they simply don't match the v18 canonical 9-column entities/technologies header.
