### Abstract

A February 1998 Aberdeen Group presentation by Chief Research Officer Peter S. Kastner analyzing the competitive position of the IBM AS/400e platform against NT Server and UNIX. The study benchmarks the AS/400e Model S40 at 25,150 tpmC and assesses its advantages and weaknesses across scalability, TCO, e-business readiness, and ISV/distributor ecosystem gaps. It concludes that the AS/400e is technically credible but faces significant perception and channel challenges heading into 1998.
