## _**InSight:**_ 

## _**Microsoft Corporation**_ 

## **Living with Windows XP Media Player and Wireless Support** 

## **November 2001** 

Microsoft's new Windows XP operating system delivers a new and high level of support to digital audio, digital video playing, digital moviemaking, and digital photography. For all of the launch marketing hype, this time Microsoft delivers. On the wireless front, XP makes connecting to wireless LANs a snap - just make sure you have on hand your Wireless Encryption Protocol (WEP) passwords converted into hexadecimal. In this _InSight_ , Aberdeen details why we believe almost all previous Windows users are going to enjoy living with Windows XP.
