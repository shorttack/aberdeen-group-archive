_**InSight:**_ 

**IT Purchasing: The View From the End of the Tunnel** 

## **August 2002** 

An analysis of the Top 20 IT company revenues shows we may be at or near the trough of the current IT recession. Aberdeen sees some significant directional changes that will become evident when spending picks up. This InSight observes key changes in IT management that must be recognized as the industry comes out of the tunnel of recession. Ignoring these changes will result in missed opportunities for IT organizations, as well as market share losses for out-of-step IT suppliers.
