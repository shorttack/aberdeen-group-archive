## _**Profile:**_ 

## _**WebEx Communications, Inc.**_ 

**WebEx: Taking Conferencing to the Business Communications Summit** 

## **April 2002** 

By focusing on building a conferencing utility-grade infrastructure designed to deliver business communications services, WebEx has pulled away to become the leader in data-rich online business meetings. The private-network infrastructure approach - uniquely used by WebEx - is ideally suited for live, data-rich business communications. 

This _Profile_ examines WebEx Meeting Center, as well as the following two specialized services: WebEx OnCall for live, remote, hands-on customer technical support and WebEx OnStage for large online events and Web seminars. A third specialized service, WebEx Training Center, rolls out in April 2002 to address the considerable market for internal training.
