## Abstract

Aberdeen profile analyzing Dell's application of its supply chain management (SCM) business model to the storage market. Covers Dell|EMC partnership (CX product family), PowerVault NAS, tape automation, storage software bundling, and service model. Argues Dell's direct model and standards focus deliver superior price/performance and TCO/ROI outcomes for customers across enterprise, midrange, and workgroup storage.
