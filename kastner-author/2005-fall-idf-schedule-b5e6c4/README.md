## Abstract

Intel Analyst Relations personalized schedule prepared by Liz Ryon for Peter Kastner (then at Vericours, Inc., Westport MA) for Fall IDF 2005 at Moscone West, San Francisco, August 23-25. Documents Kastner's access to Intel senior leadership (Otellini keynote, Maloney mobility keynote, Louis Burns digital health, Pat Gelsinger digital enterprise, Justin Rattner R&D, Mooly Eden Napa mega-briefing) and press/analyst-only Yonah architectural briefing. Preserved as employer-record artifact of Kastner's Vericours-era analyst relationship with Intel.
