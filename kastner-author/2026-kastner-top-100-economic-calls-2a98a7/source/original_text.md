results applied to study-level Phase 1 classification of the Kastner
IT Research Archive (466 high-prescience studies, 15 headline themes).

METHOD: Each theme's net attributed value (Phase 4) divided equally
across the studies classified to that theme (Phase 1). This is the
most defensible default given available data; ties within a theme
are by design and indicate the equal-allocation floor.

TOTAL: 100 of 360 high-prescience studies in 15 headline themes.
