        ABERDEEN GROUP, INC.

        Aberdeen Group is a Boston-based management consulting and computer
        market research organization.

        For technology users, Aberdeen assesses technology needs within their
        own operating and competitive context, recognizing that each
        organization’s objectives, strategy, and tactics differ. Assignments include
        enterprise-wide information architecture planning including hardware,
        software, and communications; supplier-strategy analysis; implementation
        planning; and assisting clients in clarifying the business benefits of specific
        technologies.


        PETER S. KASTNER

        Peter S. Kastner is Vice President of Aberdeen Group, Inc. He is a
        leading analyst of trends in online transaction processing (OLTP),
        client/server architectures, distributed commercial systems development,
        and TPC-A benchmark auditing and analysis.

        Prior to Aberdeen, Mr. Kastner was a marketing executive with Digital
        Equipment, Prime Computer, and Stratus Computer. As a consultant with
        Arthur D. Little Inc., Mr. Kastner was involved over a decade in dozens of
        complex systems-development projects spanning the manufacturing,
        banking and financial services, health care, and government markets, and
        including mainframe, minicomputer, and desktop systems.
