## Abstract

One-page cover letter from Peter S. Kastner (Scott-Page Associates, Westport MA) to Mark Weber, Esq. of Gibson Dunn & Crutcher in Los Angeles, following up with John Casey of Intel regarding the AMD v Intel antitrust case. Encloses a 2003 Internetweek column based on Kastner's survey of 250 companies on PC replacement buying plans, and a copy of the 1999 Intelligent Electronics v Andersen Consulting expert witness report as demonstration of his analytical style. Kastner endorses Intel's public 'affirmative defense' stance and offers services.
