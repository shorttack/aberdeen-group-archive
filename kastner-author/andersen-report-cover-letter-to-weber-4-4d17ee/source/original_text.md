RE: AMD v Intel

Dear Mark,

As a follow-up to our conversation last month with John Casey of Intel, I am enclosing two documents:
    • A column for Internetweek that reports on a survey I did in 2003 of 250 companies on their PC replacement buying plans. (The column is no longer on the web).
    • A copy of my expert witness report in the case of Intelligent Electronics v. Andersen Consulting from June 1999. The analytical style of the report displays how I approach complex technology litigation issues: what is the impact of technology on business and how do business people deal with deploying technology for business advantage.

Since we talked, I have read many of the public documents in the case, and can agree with Intel’s publicly stated conclusion that it has an affirmative defense. 

I believe that I have the experience to add value to Gibson Dunn’s team on this case.  Please contact me at the above address if you wish to discuss further how we can work together.

Best regards,



Peter S. Kastner
