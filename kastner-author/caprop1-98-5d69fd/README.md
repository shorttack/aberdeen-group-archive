## Abstract

This proposal letter to Computer Associates outlines Aberdeen Group's plan to produce three separate Aberdeen Profile publications supporting CA's Unicenter TNG and interBiz product lines, tailored to distinct audiences: CA customers, channel partners, and press/analysts/Wall Street. Aberdeen recommends against a single publication due to the diversity of CA's target audiences, and proposes an on-site briefing to review disclosed materials before drafting. The engagement includes research, reprint production (20,000 copies each), electronic distribution rights, and licensing for CA to reproduce additional paper copies.
