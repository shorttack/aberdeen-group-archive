## Original Document Text

July 23, 1999
Lisa Ann Savino
Divisional Assistant VP
Computer Associates International, Inc.
1 Computer Associates Plaza Islandia, NY 11788-7000 516-342-3304

Dear Lisa Ann,
It was good to see again at CA World 99 in New Orleans. I was pleased by the press and analyst reception to the rollout of the interBiz solutions.

As we discussed earlier this month, Aberdeen's next step is to prepare a white paper on the business benefits of CA's interBiz initiative, and how interBiz solutions will enable a new class of managed business processes.

This proposal details how Aberdeen can proceed with such a project.

We are looking forward to this exciting project, and to participating in the Las Vegas conference next month.

Regards,
Peter Kastner Chief Research Officer Aberdeen Group, Inc.

Aberdeen is pleased to submit the following proposal for your consideration.

BACKGROUND AND PROJECT SCOPE
Aberdeen has been asked to provide its independent analysis of Computer Associates' market position, core products, key technologies, sales and marketing strategies, alliances and partnerships, and future directions in the application infrastructure space. The anchor for these observations will be the recently announced interBiz solution suite and underlying bizWorks infrastructure.

Aberdeen proposes delivering its analysis by means of a custom consulting engagement. As part of this engagement, Aberdeen has met with key Computer Associates executives to get a better understanding of the company's plans and to provide our immediate thoughts and feedback. Aberdeen will then deliver its analysis to Computer Associates management in the form of an Aberdeen Profile.

Computer Associates would then have the option of using the Profile as part of its corporate and marketing message. In the past, Aberdeen clients have found Profiles valuable across a wide range of audiences, including sales prospects, investors, strategic partners, distributors and new staff. In the field, a Profile educates potential clients on a supplier's products, technology, partnerships, and strategic vision. Getting a company's message across effectively is crucial in opening up new accounts and recruiting partners and distributors.

Because a Profile is a high-impact analysis document, Aberdeen reflects the most recent strategic developments and new product directions. We also include tables and diagrams that provide a summary of product characteristics and benefits.

PROJECT APPROACH
As part of this consulting engagement, Aberdeen will review existing product information and discuss Computer Associates' offerings with key members of your staff. Aberdeen will then put its findings and analysis in the form of an Aberdeen Profile on Computer Associates. This Profile, which will be 4 to 8 pages, will then be sent to Computer Associates' senior management for review and comment. Should Computer Associates then choose, Aberdeen will reprint the Profile on its stock and ship the reprints to Computer Associates for distribution.

DURATION AND COST
The work outlined above will be carried out over a four-week period with a goal of shipping the finished reprints to CA by the middle of August in preparation for the Executive Forum in Las Vegas. Aberdeen is prepared to start work on the Profile immediately. We would like to deliver the first draft of the Profile to you by August 2nd.

Our professional services fee for this consulting project is $15,000. The fee includes an electronic version and a Web license, and we will also supply 25,000 reprint copies of the Computer Associates Profile. This fee includes time spent interviewing Computer Associates' staff and customers, putting our analysis in Profile format, and will be counted as one of the publications Aberdeen committed to in our agreement dated November 20, 1998.

Additional reprints of the completed Computer Associates Profile are available at a discounted price of $.60 each. Profiles are printed only on distinctive Profile stationery by Aberdeen and Aberdeen does not authorize reprinting by others. Shipping charges and applicable sales tax will be added to all orders. The project professional service fees will be billed upon sending the first draft of the Profile to Computer Associates.

GENERAL PROVISIONS
In the past, Aberdeen Group has carried out a considerable amount of technical and marketing research in the field covered by this proposal. This experience qualifies us to undertake the assignment defined above, and we expect to continue such work in the future.

To protect your interests, we agree that any confidential information that we develop or have furnished to us by you during the work will be safeguarded in accordance with our established professional standards. Furthermore, it is our practice in considering assignments to decline them whenever it appears to us at all probable that our experience with a past or present client might be inappropriate in the new assignment.

Any Profiles, Viewpoints, White Papers or other publication that result from this assignment remain the property of Aberdeen Group. Aberdeen owns the copyright and other intellectual property rights of the Profile as noted above, but Computer Associates will receive a review copy of the Profile before it is produced.

STAFF RESPONSIBILITY
Peter Kastner will be in supervisory charge of the project. Joining him will be Bob Moran, research VP, and Guy Creese, a senior analyst in the decision support practice.

ACCEPTANCE
We will accept a signed copy of this letter as a contractual document authorizing us to proceed with the work. Alternatively, we will accept a Computer Associates verbal purchase order followed by a written purchase order.

Yours most truly,
Peter Kastner
Chief Research Officer
Aberdeen Group, Inc.
