## Abstract

Revised engagement letter from Aberdeen Group to Maxtor CMO Stephen DiFranco for the "Pools of Storage" / midline storage market outreach program. Covers four phases: strategy workshop, decision tool development, message testing, and press evangelism. Total project cost $114,000 over ~10 weeks. Introduces the four-pool ILM storage lexicon (on-line, mid-line, near-line, off-line) and deliverables including interactive decision tools, white paper, and US/Europe road shows.
