Company Facts
Founded in 1982, Adobe has grown to become the second largest PC software company.  The company is a leader in network publishing, word and media content authoring, and document management.  These products are used by web and graphics professionals, publishers and designers, business users and consumers.
Products and Services
Adobe’s wide range of individual products and product collections can be categorized as: content design, production, and publishing; digital video; digital imaging; and document rendering technologies such as fonts, print rendering, and ePaper.
