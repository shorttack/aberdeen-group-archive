## Abstract

These are our current recommendation for mainstream desktop personal computers that offer the best combination of price, value, and longevity is: • Microprocessor: Pentium 4 at 2.4 GHz (533 MHz front-side bus) • Memory: 256 MB DDR SDRAM (minimum) • Hard Drive: 7200 RPM 20 GB or better. (We price 40 GB for consistency) • Optical: CD-ROM • Floppy: Optional. (We favor USB flash memory drives in 2003) • Monitor: 15" or 17" XGA-resolution LCD flat panel • Communications: 1
