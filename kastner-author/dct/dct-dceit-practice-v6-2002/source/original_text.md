Cable Labs
      Cable Labs is an industry consortium that
      develops specifications for equipment and
      services to increase cable revenues and insure
      interoperability.         Projects include:

• Packet CableTM: Standards to enable Internet protocol
  services to be delivered over cable, including VoIP and
  real-time multimedia services
• Open CableTM: A standard defining a point-of-
  deployment module (POD) which will customize a
  consumer purchased STB/Gateway to the local cable
  suppliers specific, proprietary features

• Cable HomeTM: A specification that links home

  residential gateway that supports:
      – Self configuration

      – QoS management
      – Ubiquitous networking

The Home Networking Market is Beginning to Grow



• Currently, home networking is driven by a desire for:
       – PC/peripheral connectivity
       – Sharing of broadband connectivity
• Category 5 wiring/ethernet is used in recent
  construction, but technologies that do not require
  additional wiring are now being deployed
• These new technologies provide the basis for the
  broadband connectivity essential to future applications
  such as ubiquitous streaming audio and video
