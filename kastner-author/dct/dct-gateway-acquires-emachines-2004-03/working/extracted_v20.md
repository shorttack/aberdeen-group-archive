## _**Hot Topic:**_ 

## **Gateway's Acquisition of eMachines Bodes Well** 

## **February 3, 2004** 

In a bid to shore up its sagging fortunes, personal computer maker Gateway announced that it would buy privately held eMachines in a $235 million deal. Aberdeen sees this as a shrewd move on Gateway's part. With this deal, Gateway expands its distribution channel, opening new channels in which its innovative products may be well received.
