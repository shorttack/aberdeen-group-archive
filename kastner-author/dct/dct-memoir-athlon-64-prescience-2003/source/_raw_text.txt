Subject: Prescience Call on AMD Athlon 64 (Sept 2003)

The commentary keeps recommending Athlon XP as the value play through August 2003
without anticipating the 64-bit transition. AMD was way early on 64-bit as OS support
was years away. It was not a compelling buying factor at time of purchase and maybe
even at end of product life for an August 2003 purchase.
