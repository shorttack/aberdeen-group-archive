## Abstract

News analysis of Microsoft's announcement of Windows XP Media Center Edition — a living-room PC variant combining TV tuner, hardware video encoder, and infrared remote, first demoed at CES 2002 under the 'Freestyle' codename. HP, NEC, and Samsung are the launch OEMs; Kastner frames the offering as the strongest PC-in-the-living-room push to date while noting Windows task-scheduling vulnerabilities and competition from SonicBlue and TiVo.
