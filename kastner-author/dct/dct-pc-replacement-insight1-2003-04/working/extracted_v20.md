_**InSight:**_ **PC Replacements: Lawyers, Auditors, and Common Sense Rules** 

## **May 7, 2003** 

The economic recession has stalled PC replacements at many pennypinching enterprises because the old machines are still operating. Now, time has caught up with Microsoft's operating system support life cycle. In the next eight months, Windows NT 4.xx and Windows 98/SE -- installed on over 50 million business desktops around the world -- will no longer get updates or security patches. Prudent businesses should consider accelerating their PC replacements. The good news is, this is an excellent time to start planning a PC acquisition cycle.
