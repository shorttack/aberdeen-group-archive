## Original Document Text

﻿PIT Service Offering


Target Companies

Consumer Electronic Firms (CE): Sony, Matsushita, Samsung, Phillips, Lucky Gold
Computer Companies: Microsoft, HP, Dell, IBM, Apple, Gateway, E-Machines, Sony
Cable Equipment Companies: Motorola, Scientific Atlanta
WiFi-x  Firms: over 20
Home PNA Adherents

Secondary Targets
Retail CE & PC: Best Buy, Circuit City, CompUSA, Fries
ILECS: SBC, BellSouth, Verizon


Tiered Service Offering

    1) Basic: Includes Core slide deck covering PIT in five categories; 
    • Gateways
    • Media Servers/PC-CE convergence
    • Networks (WiFi-x and wire line)
    • Displays/UI/Usability
    •  Internet/Cable enablement.

Initial phone or Boston Office meeting for 2+ hours. Review client materials and then present core deck and critique client approach.

Quarterly webcast to present updated Core Deck. Questions by email or follow-up phone.

Two Retainer days can be used for:

	-Review of marketing collateral
-Deeper analysis of/presentation on category above of focused interest
	-Speaking Engagements
	-Trade Show Participation
	- Consulting workshop or custom consulting

Pricing  $19,500

    2) Basic plus Analysis of top 3 Contenders in the 5 Categories for a maximun   of 15 analyses. Company list reviewed every 6 months, content reviewed Quarterly. Breaking  news bulletins as appropriate. Note, a Profile can be purchased for the web, but not a Contender Analysis. We choose them on merit.

Pricing $24,500




3)  Tier 2 plus variable number of retainer days that can be applied to custom consulting


	Pricing $24,500 + $4,000 per diem plus expenses.
