About Aberdeen Group PITAS
Aberdeen Group’s Personal Information Technology Awareness Service is a powerful combination of offerings that provide market visibility to knowledgeable and qualified buyers, support for outbound communications and a safe forum for testing critical ideas before they are delivered to market.
PITAS Deliverables
The Aberdeen PITAS program for (COMPANY NAME) during 2002 consists of:
    • An Aberdeen Group Snapshot of your company, hosted by Aberdeen Group on its Website;
    • Objective, business focused quotes for your press announcements;
    • Aberdeen analyst reference support for your company, its products and services, via telephone, for the press, financial industry, and private investors; and
    • Bi-annual, one-hour long briefings.
Supplier Snapshot
We will ask that you fill out a brief questionnaire and mail it back to us.  We will then format a Supplier Snapshot for you to review for accuracy before being posted to Aberdeen’s Website.  The Snapshot will be available free-of-charge to all persons visiting Aberdeen’s Website.
Press Quotes
A reasonable number of requests for quotes for use with your Company’s outbound press announcements will be supported.  Press quotes will only be objective assessments of the business impact of your company and its products.  
Quotes for publication must be approved by Aberdeen prior to issuance.
Analyst Reference
A reasonable number of inquiries will be accepted concerning your company and its products — via telephone—from members of the press, private financiers, and members of the public financial industry.  The telephone inquiry service is restricted to short, 10-to-15 minutes conversations.  
Scheduled Briefings
Up to two (2), one-hour long consultative briefing sessions may be scheduled.  These sessions can take place at our offices or via the phone.  During these sessions you will be provided with critical feedback including market findings and analysis relevant to your company, its positioning, products, messaging, channels, and plans.
Cost and Duration
The period covered by this agreement is one (1) year, commencing Month xx, 2002.  
The professional service fee for this offering is $2,500 payable upon acceptance of this agreement.  
Terms are net 30 days.  In all cases, and if required, travel, sales tax and shipping/courier expenses will be invoiced at cost.  
A 1% interest charge will be added to any payment exceeding 30 days past due.   
Snapshot Reprint Distribution Option and Fees
In addition to Aberdeen hosting the Supplier Snapshot of your company on our Website, you may obtain reprints of the Snapshot at any time during the coming year.  Our fees for reprints of the Snapshot are $1.00 per reprint.  Invoices for reprints will be issued upon the delivery of each order.  
Aberdeen Group does not authorize reprinting by others.  
PITAS RENEWAL
We will automatically renew the Personal Information Technology Awareness Service program on your behalf for a subsequent year, upon the completion of the prior year of service.  If you decide that you do not want to renew the service, simply return the invoice, marked “CANCELLED”, to us within 30 days after your receipt of the invoice, to our Boston office at the following address:  Aberdeen Group, P.O. Box 847370 Boston, MA 02284-7370.
GENERAL PROVISIONS
In the past, Aberdeen Group has carried out a considerable amount of technical, marketing research and consulting in the field covered by this proposal.  This experience qualifies us to undertake the assignment defined above, and we expect to continue such work in the future.
To protect your interests, we agree that any confidential information that we develop, or information we have furnished to us by you during the work will be safeguarded in accordance with our established professional standards.  
Information we obtain from you and your customers that is in the public domain may be published in our reports, including Aberdeen Profiles, InSights, Snapshots, White Papers, Viewpoints, Impacts, and Research Reports, and other Aberdeen publications becomes our property.  Further, Aberdeen owns the copyright to all Aberdeen Group publications and enforces its copyright.
Aberdeen represents and warrants that it shall use commercially reasonable best efforts to provide services in conformance with generally accepted standards of good practice and all applicable laws, rules, and regulations.
In no event (i) shall Aberdeen’s liability for all damages exceed actual direct damages caused by the specific service complained of or (ii) shall Aberdeen’s maximum liability for all damages exceed the amount actually paid to Aberdeen by a client for the services rendered or (iii) shall Aberdeen be liable for incidental, consequential, exemplary, special or indirect damages (including but not limited to lost business profits, revenue, goodwill and other indirect damages), even if Aberdeen has been advised of the possibility of the same.
Furthermore, it is our practice in considering assignments to decline them whenever it appears to us at all probable that our experience with current client might be inappropriate in the new assignment.
STAFF RESPONSIBILITY
The principal analysts of Aberdeen’s personal information technology practice, Peter Kastner, Ethan Cohen and Isaac Ro, will be responsible for delivering the PITAS service  
ACCEPTANCE
