## Original Document Text

﻿Personal Electronic Technology Vision 

Timing: this fall to 3+ years out

Scope: Personal electronic technology used at home, in transit, and at work

Document Structure:

    • Graphical depiction of overall Vision at low resolution


    • Research Approach: Identify End User Requirements/Desires. Work closely with Vendors/emergent vendors to discuss approaches/possibilities. Hypothesize products/approaches to deliver user desires/requirements. Test and refine hypotheses.


    • Statement of Principles

--Plug and Play Imperative

--Need for /benefit of upgradability

--Customer support model


    • Illustrative User Requirements

--Capture, edit, store, display, and share (email, file transfer) personal still and motion video
--Access the home network/internet from anywhere in the house
--Display/play content (audio, video) anywhere in the house on demand from a central repository
--Access corporate and home networks and internet with portable devices when in those locations and in transit
--Program car stereo from central audio repository

    • Home personal electronics


--Requirements

-Networking
	-High speed: wireless entertainment and productivity tools (PCs, printers, phones, etc)
	-Low speed: appliance control and status reporting, security

-Copy protection

-Conditional Access to premium content

Implications for Sony

    • Competitive Threats

    • What Sony Has to Get Right

--Good user experience for all products
--Value added systems of products enabling value pricing
	--Seamless Plug and Play
	--Superb customer support
	--Extended asset life through field upgrades/downloads (for a fee)

    • Business Process Issues/Models

--Supply chain

--Customer support

--Channels
	--High end CE channels (Tweeter, home theater) for demos/missonary sales
	--Turnkey system installation?
	--Category confusion around system components/get system shelf space

	--Financing system sales

--Installed base marketing
