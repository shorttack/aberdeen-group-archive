## Abstract

Amidst the weekend of turkey, stuffing, and crazed holiday shopping, the deals to pounce on arrived. In fact, the weekly offers arrived Thanksgiving Day amidst the department store flyers and announcements of holiday savings. These huge post-Thanksgiving Day offers mean the best bargains we have seen in six months, but also mean more work for the consumer to track more sources than usual.
