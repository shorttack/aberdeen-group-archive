Summary of New Technologies for Workstation 
Performance
• DDR2: Scalable bandwidth, lower power consumption, higher 
density memory technology to meet present and future needs
• PCI Express: Fast, easily scalable, physically compact, point-to-
point, bi-directional data interconnect benefits customers by 
favorably impacting many aspects of workstation architecture.
• SATA RAID: High-performance, integrated SATA RAID 
controller for massive local storage at a competitive price point
• EM64T: Maximized memory capacity for P4 CPUs and 925X 
chipset (max. 4GB), and Xeon CPUs (max. 16GB) WS yielding 
uncompromised 32-bit performance and simultaneous operation 
of 64-bit applications.
