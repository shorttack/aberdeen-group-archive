Market-wide Point of View
Service organizations just beginning a major cost restructuring a la U.S. manufacturing 30 years ago
IT service sector particularly affected as buyers demand that their service providers arbitrage labor, collaborate, decrease overall cost and improve value proposition

Companies seeking to find lower cost resources themselves
Integrators seeing no movement in large, long timeframe work

Larger providers and ERP vendors re-discovering all-inclusive hosted solutions as the means to deliver value, flexibility, transformation and scalable solutions

### Abstract

Aberdeen discussion deck prepared for Dell executives analyzing the restructuring of IT professional services. Argues services are beginning a major cost restructuring mirroring US manufacturing 30 years prior, driven by offshore labor arbitrage, BPO/On Demand account control, and ERP-vendor hosted solutions. Proposes a four-stage partner-ecosystem maturity model and asks where Dell sits on that continuum.
