Omnigy meets market desire for a rapid time to
market,
low investment “commerce platform”
Interviews indicate that the market wants the time to market and implementation
benefits offered by Omnigy

Comprehensive and low capital investment
“We are looking at Ascendant because we want a comprehensive, turnkey solution. We want a front-end
that is turnkey, and ASP, so that the capital expenditure is lower. The question is: ‘is it doable within the
context of a reasonable cost structure and suite of services’?”
Doug Witt, FedEx
“I very much like that Omnigy is offered on an ASP basis… I like ASP because of the low up front cost
and the fact that it is a scalable system that can expand as we grow.”
JC Dupuy, Simplexis
Easy implementation
“ASP solutions are good because of the easy implementation.  We are already telling our clients to go to
ASP… Most clients are lousy at implementing [point solutions].  It is crazy because that is where they
need help the most, but they don’t ask for it.”
Bill Kuipers, Spaide Kuipers
“Our criteria in evaluating solutions was: 1) affordable off the rack e-commerce store; 2) safe and
secure; 3) integrates well into our distribution systems.”
Kristen Chapman, G-Push

The market wants broad functionality,
and is not necessarily tied to full service providers
The market wants broad functionality in their commerce platforms, especially in the
back-end
“We had an online store, but there were lots of manual steps in order processing. We wanted to
move to the next level... The front-end (CRM and shopping cart) will be the complex piece.
[Omnigy’s capabilities] are a tremendous benefit for us.”
Scott Harris, Toy Craze
“I see Omnigy as key for third party fulfilling.  It has some capabilities that we don’t have today in
the front-end around the shopping cart, credit cards and processing payments… and we don’t have
a system to deal with drop shippers”
Cameron McKennitt, United Stationers
Companies looking for full service providers (fulfillment and systems) would have
been happy with an Ascendant-fulfiller partnership
“This is a trial program for Duraflame. I wanted one package; that is to outsource the whole service
to a third party… Sure, I would consider a partner situation.”
Chris Caron, Duraflame
“We wanted a Web solution/e-commerce solution that included fulfillment. We wanted someone
who could provide a complete solution - soup to nuts… We didn’t go with Ascendant because of
their move to systems. We would have had to deal with more players than we wanted… A close
partnership - sure, that might have helped.”
David McGuir, Hippo
