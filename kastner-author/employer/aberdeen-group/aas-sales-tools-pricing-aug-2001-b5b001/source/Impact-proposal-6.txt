BACKGROUND AND PROJECT SCOPE
An Impact is an Aberdeen Group analyst’s synopsis of how a supplier’s new announcement or product introduction will affect a market and that supplier’s future prospects.  It presents Aberdeen’s bottom-line findings, succinctly stating why an ITS decision-maker should be knowledgeable about [COMPANY]’s action.  An Impact analyzes both the customer impact and the impact within a well-defined market segment.  Clients find Impacts highly effective for use in press kits, direct mailings, and up-to-the-minute sales collateral.
PROJECT APPROACH
Aberdeen will provide its findings and analysis in the form of an Aberdeen Impact on [COMPANY].  An Aberdeen Impact is delivered as a two-page document printed on Aberdeen Impact paper stock and also delivered in HTML and PDF formats.  Aberdeen’s analysis will be prepared based on a single two to four hour briefing (the “Research Meeting”) and supporting materials and presentations that [COMPANY] will provide to the Aberdeen analyst.  
Prior to printing, a draft of the written Impact will be sent to [COMPANY]’s senior management for a single round of review and comments.  After revisions, a final draft will then be sent to [COMPANY] for sign-off.  
Should [COMPANY]’s product or company positioning change after Aberdeen has commenced writing the Impact, or should [COMPANY] requests a change in emphasis which requires significant re-writing of the work product after or during the preparation of the draft report, this request shall be considered as extra work outside the original scope of this agreement.  In such circumstances, Aberdeen and [COMPANY] shall mutually agree upon the redefined scope and any associated additional Research Fees.
