## Abstract

Undated Aberdeen Group corporate company profile documenting the post-Kastner leadership era: James Bedard CEO, Stephen Gold President & CMO, Mark Vigoroso Chief Research Officer. Peter Kastner appears on the Board of Directors, listed as 'Director, Vericours, Inc.' -- the first canonical source documenting Kastner's Vericours role after his Aberdeen tenure. John Logan (Aberdeen co-founder) is listed as CEO of Obian, foreshadowing the Logan-Kastner path from Aberdeen through Obian. Date approximated to ~2008-2010 based on leadership composition. Profile touts 125,000+ research community, 260,000 leads delivered, research used by 90%+ of Fortune 1000.
