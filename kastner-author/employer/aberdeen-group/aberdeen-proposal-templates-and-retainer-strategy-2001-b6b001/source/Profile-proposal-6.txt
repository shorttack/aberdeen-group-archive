BACKGROUND AND PROJECT SCOPE

In the past, Aberdeen clients have found Profiles valuable across a wide range of audiences including sales prospects, investors, strategic partners, distributors, and new staff.  In the field, a Profile educates potential clients on a supplier's products, value proposition, technology, partnerships, and strategic vision.  Delivering a company's message effectively is crucial in opening up new accounts as well as recruiting partners and distributors.
Because a Profile is a high-impact analysis medium, Aberdeen focuses on the most recent strategic developments and new product directions.  We also include tables and diagrams that provide a summary of product characteristics and benefits.
Aberdeen will provide this assessment based on interviews with [COMPANY] and with [COMPANY]’s customers.  These Profile incorporates these interviews with Aberdeen’s own perspective garnered from its continuing business of providing industry analysis to the Information Technology community.
