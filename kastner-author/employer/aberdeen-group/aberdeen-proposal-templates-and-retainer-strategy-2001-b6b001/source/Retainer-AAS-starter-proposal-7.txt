Aberdeen Advisory Service (AAS) 
INTRODUCTORY PACKAGE
Aberdeen Advisory Service is a multi-faceted service that serves as the basis for our on-going relationship.  The service puts Aberdeen Group's principal analyst-consultants in direct, one-on-one contact with key decision-making client executives — when they are needed.  Aberdeen Advisory Service not only provides [COMPANY] with access to Aberdeen’s senior analysts covering [TOPIC], but also can take a cross-disciplinary approach to include interaction with Aberdeen’s entire research staff when required.  

The value of the Aberdeen Advisory Service is the ad hoc access to our market-research analyst-experts, so that the day-to-day “what about?” questions can be asked and answered — generating the timely, market-influencing response so necessary in today’s tough and fast-moving markets.

The AAS Service consists of the following:

Designated Analyst As Relationship Manager — [ANALYST NAME], [ANALYST TITLE] will be the day-to-day AAS relationship manager for [COMPANY].  [DESCRIBE QUALIFICATIONS FOR TOPIC AREA]  [HIS/HER]	 biography is attached as Appendix A.

Briefings — Aberdeen Group will hold a one- to two-hour briefing with [COMPANY].  The purpose of this interactive session is to provide you with Aberdeen’s latest research findings in the [TOPIC] market and discuss their relevance to [COMPANY].  Aberdeen is also available to provide its feedback regarding [COMPANY]’s latest corporate, product, and technology initiatives, typically through a review of the corporate presentation.  These sessions are typically conducted either by phone or at Aberdeen’s offices, but can be arranged as an on-site meeting at extra cost.

Telephone and Email Access — Reasonable telephone or email access is provided to the designated Aberdeen Group analyst by two contacts at the client company.   Access is most commonly used for ad hoc discussions on market conditions, competitors, or strategy options — situations where instant analysis can help direct critical decision-making.

Public Relations and Press Interaction — Aberdeen Group will make itself available as a creditable third-party source to assist your public relations and press release efforts with appropriate objective quotes and background briefings.

Trade Show Access — Aberdeen Group will meet privately with clients to share observations and perspectives at all relevant trade shows that Aberdeen attends in the [TOPIC] technology area.  Typically, we would walk the show floor with you and discuss the competition’s trade-show positioning as well as any hot new entrants.

Aberdeen Research Publications — You will have access through a portal at Aberdeen.com to the published research and analysis of the [TOPIC] technology practice.  This forms the basis for our intellectual discussions on market dynamics, trends, and competition.

One-Half Day of Consulting Time — The AAS introductory package includes one-half day of consulting time in our offices with your analyst.    The analyst’s services can include standardized and customized engagements.  For example, in one or more interactive Consulting Sessions with an Aberdeen Analyst (or several analysts in converging markets), we can provide [COMPANY] with suggestions and recommendations on market strategy, market positioning, and product rollout presentations and messaging — as well what messages need to be communicated to IT and line-of-business decision-makers and market influencers such as Wall Street firms, industry analysts, and the press.  [COMPANY] may also ask Aberdeen to evaluate and review marketing and sales presentations and provide our field-research insights on current user buying patterns.  

The one-half day of consulting time can be used all at once, split over several occasions, or applied as a labor credit against a larger engagement.  An uplift is available for on-site consulting to reflect our travel time.

Preferential Aberdeen Consulting Rates — Advisory Service clients receive preferential rates for Aberdeen Group Consulting Services.  Any member of the Aberdeen Research Staff is available to AAS clients on a preferred-rate basis.
