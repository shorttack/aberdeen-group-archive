BACKGROUND AND PROJECT OBJECTIVE
An Executive White Paper provides an understanding of industry trends, technologies, and methodologies — with an emphasis on the business implications for end-user organizations.  An Executive White Paper describes and analyzes a key IT issue and how a specific supplier responds to it.  Aberdeen Group publishes an Executive White Paper when an in-depth analysis is required for a significant issue that is being vigorously debated within the industry.
Aberdeen is often requested to test and validate a marketing or technical positioning proposition believed by the sponsoring client to establish and validate a unique and compelling competitive advantage.  The client and Aberdeen agree on this positioning thesis, which will be researched and tested.  The results of Aberdeen Group’s research are documented in the form of an Executive White Paper.
PROJECT APPROACH
For [COMPANY], Aberdeen proposes writing an Executive White Paper that articulates [COMPANY]’s market positioning and true value proposition to key potential customers and end-users of your core technologies.  Ideally, Aberdeen would interview one or more [COMPANY] customers with a view toward identifying and characterizing approaches that will result in the greatest benefits and fewest pitfalls.  
Task 1 – On-Site Research & Consultation
To conduct the corporate/product background research effectively, Aberdeen would like to visit [COMPANY]’s offices to speak specifically with key management personnel in the form of a “Research Day” to be able to complete the Executive White Paper.  During the Research Meeting, we expect to review in depth [COMPANY]’s market strategy, positioning, messages and PR strategy, and channels and alliances.  This typically centers around [COMPANY]’s own corporate and sales presentations.  While the scope of this project is not go-to-market strategy consulting, we will endeavor to optimize and clarify [COMPANY]’s strategy and technology benefits within the time available during the Research Meeting so that the story can be effectively communicated in the Executive White Paper.
We ask that you provide Aberdeen with a set of existing product presentation materials used to describe and market your products to prospective customers prior to this session. In addition, we request that you provide Aberdeen with two to four customer names and contacts for the purpose of being interviewed by Aberdeen.  Aberdeen will conduct these interviews by phone to validate value propositions, customer support and product performance.  Aberdeen reserves the right to identify and interview additional customers independently.
Task 2 – Analysis and Executive White Paper Writing
In the first phase of development, a single draft of the written Executive White Paper will be sent to [COMPANY]’s senior management for a single round of review and comment.  After revisions are identified, a final draft will be sent to [COMPANY] for approval and sign-off.  
Should [COMPANY]’s product or company positioning change after Aberdeen has commenced writing the Executive White Paper, or should [COMPANY] requests a change in emphasis which requires significant re-writing after or during the preparation of the draft report, this request shall be considered as extra work outside the original scope of this agreement.  In such circumstances, Aberdeen and [COMPANY] shall mutually agree upon the redefined scope and any additional Research Fees. 
Task 3 – Media Production
An Aberdeen Executive White Paper is a written document, 5 to 16 pages in length, printed on Aberdeen paper stock and/or delivered electronically in HTML/PDF text formatted as an Aberdeen document for display on the WWW.   

Prior to printing, a draft of the Executive White Paper will be sent to [COMPANY]’s senior management for a single round of review and comments.  After revisions, a final draft will then be sent to [COMPANY] for sign-off.    
Should [COMPANY]’s product or company positioning change after Aberdeen has commenced writing the Executive White Paper, or should [COMPANY] requests a change in emphasis which requires significant re-writing of the work product after or during the preparation of the draft report, this request shall be considered as extra work outside the original scope of this agreement.  In such circumstances, Aberdeen and [COMPANY] shall mutually agree upon the redefined scope and any additional Research Fees.
