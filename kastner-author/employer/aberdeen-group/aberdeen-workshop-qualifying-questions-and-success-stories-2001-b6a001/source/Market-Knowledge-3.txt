Market Knowledge Workshop 
										Qualifying Questions
Market Overview - Does the client understand the overall market dynamics for their product/service?
    • Can you define for me the market that you compete within? (US / Global)
    • What are the major sub-components that make up your market?
    • What is your stated market strategy? 
    • What do you perceive to be the current dollar size of the market?
    • Which product markets does your product currently interface with? 
    • How fast do you perceive the market growing?
    • What do you perceive to be driving market growth?
    • What is your 3-5 year vision on market evolution? Will the market be prey or predator?
    • Of the list of major industry players currently not in the market, who do you believe is likely to enter the market over the next 18 months?
Competitive Analysis -- Does the client understand the competitive landscape? 
    • Who do you believe to be your top three competitors?  Why?
    • Who do you perceive to be your segment specific or niche competitors?
    • Can you tell me what differentiates you from your competitors? 
    • Who do you believe to be your “future” competitors and why?
    • What are the top five functional strengths of your product compared to your competitors?
    • What are the five biggest market / client challenges?
    • Who have you lost to recently in a competitive bid?  Why?
    • What competitors have you displaced recently?

Market Landscape – Can the client describe their market segmentation? 
    • Describe your market segmentation?
    • Which market segments will you penetrate first and why?
    • What do you perceive to be the market size by sub component segment?

Current Company / Product Position – Does the client know where they fit in the market? 
    • Can you describe for me where you feel you fit into the market?
    • What is your market strategy?
    • What do you think will happen to your product in the next 6-12 months?
    • What are the top corporate sales concerns?
    • What type of technical environment is best suited for your product?
    • What vertical industries are the most appropriate for your product?
    • Do you know what clients are lacking most after they go “live” with your product?

Market Trends – Does the client understand the current market trends?
    • Can you describe for me the top three market trends in your market?
    • What are the top three pricing trends?
    • What are the top three product trends?
    • What are the top three service trends?
    • What are the top three distribution trends?
    • What are the top three objections you encounter to purchasing your solution?
    • What is your 3-5 year vision on market evolution?

Prepare for the Future – Does the client have a solid marketing strategy based upon their “accurate” understanding of the market?
    • Based on your perception of the market, what is your strategy going forward?
