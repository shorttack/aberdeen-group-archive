Messaging Strategy Workshop 
Qualifying Questions

Messaging Overview – Does the client have a strategy for communicating their product to the market?
    • Describe for me your communication plan to amplify your message to the market?
    • Who are you working with on your messaging strategy?  
    • Do you have a separate communication plan for your channel distributors?
    • How do you plan to maximize your opportunities for your message to be heard?
    • How well are your internal resources and agency aligned on your message?
    • What are some of the roadblocks you’ve encountered or anticipate encountering in your messaging strategy?

Current Communication Channels for Message – Is the clients existing communication strategy “viable”?
    • What field market research about your customers supports your strategy?
    • How well do you think your current communications strategy has been working?
    • What have some of the roadblocks/problems been with your current messaging strategy?
    • Do you have any ideas of how you can overcome some of these roadblocks/problems?
    • How are your competitors reacting to your messaging?
Leverage Ad/PR Agency? – Is the client getting maximum value from their Ad/PR agency? 
    • Are you getting the results you want from your Ad/PR agency?
    • Do you feel your Ad/PR agency could be doing more?  What else would you want them to do?
    • Do you feel your Ad/PR agency is aligned with your marketing strategy?
    • How compelling do you think your written PR/Ad material is?
    • How compelling are your messages written?
    • What are the results of your latest ad campaign?
    • What web marketing campaigns have your done?
Internal Staff Alignment with Sales, PR and Ad Agencies to Strategic Positioning – Are the clients resources aligned appropriately based on their market strategy?  
    • Is your sales team aligned with your market strategy? 
    • Do you feel that your sales team is getting up to speed fast enough?
    • Can all your customer-facing employees give the same elevator pitch about your company and products?
Effective Vehicles to Communicate Message – Is the client effectively utilizing all marketing tools to communicate their message?
    • What types of marketing tools are you using?
    • What type of marketing tools has worked best for you?  Least?
