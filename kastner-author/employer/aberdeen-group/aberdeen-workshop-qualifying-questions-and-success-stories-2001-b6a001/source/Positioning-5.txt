Positioning Strategy Workshop 
									Qualifying Questions

Positioning Overview – Does the client have a “viable” market position? 
    • Describe for me your current market position?
    • Can you define for me the market that you compete within? (US / Global)
    • What are the major sub-components that make up your market?
    • What is your stated market strategy? 
    • What do you perceive to be the current dollar size of the market?
    • Which product markets does your product currently interface with? 
    • How fast do you perceive the market growing?
    • What do you perceive to be driving market growth?
    • What is your 3-5 year vision on market evolution? Will the market be prey or predator?
    • Of the list of major industry players currently not in the market, who do you believe is likely to enter the market over the next 18 months?

Current Position  -- Does the client have a “realistic” understanding of where the market perceives them to be positioned?
    • What is the perception in the market as to how you are positioned?
    • Do you think your company has the capabilities to meet the market needs?  Why?

Positioning Against Market Needs?
    • Can you tell me what your customers are asking for?
    • What are your product capabilities in relation to what the market is asking for?
    • What types of products does the market want?  Features?
    • Where do you meet?  Where net?
    • What is the perception in the market as to how you are positioned?
    • Do you think your company has the capabilities to meet the market needs?  Why?
Positioning Against Competitors – Does the client understand the strengths and weakness of their competitors?
    • What are you competitors doing?
    • Can you tell mew what differentiates you from your competitor’s offerings?
    • What are the top five functional strengths of your product compared to you competitors?
    • Can you describe the weaknesses of some of your competitors?
    • What are the five biggest market / client challenges?
    • Who have you lost to recently in a competitive bid?  Why?

Recommended Positioning
    • Can you define for me what your new position / message will be?
