
Communicating Aberdeen’s New Business Model



The communication for Aberdeen’s new business model will be a three-tier approach:



    I. Company wide overview

        ◦ Provide a forty-five minute overview of the “new” business model to all employees at an upcoming company wide staff meeting.
        ◦ Distribute new business model to all employees.


    II. Research Training

        ◦ Schedule research training for each major technology group (CEC, EBA, Network Services, Platforms) to review specific details of our new positioning, branding and product/services model.
        ◦ Training will include role-playing “elevator pitch” and sales vignette scenarios.
        ◦ Distribute supporting material for reference.


    III. Company Marketing Collateral (Follow-up)

        ◦ Develop streaming media / video vignettes describing Aberdeen’s position, branding and business model.
        ◦ Personalize by having Tom and/or Practice Directors participate.
