Thank you for your continued interest and support.  Per our discussions, Aberdeen Group is pleased to present the following proposal of five Retainer Services for your consideration.

Background
Aberdeen Group offers both Consulting Services and Strategic Analysis Retainer Services.  As you know from your experience with us, our Consulting Services range from White Papers, Market Surveys and Sales Force Education Seminars to extensive Management Consulting Engagements.  However, you may not be as familiar with our Retainer Services that are listed here by Knowledge Center:

- Midrange Computing

Retainer Service Components
Each of the Aberdeen Group Retainer Services offers the following components:

Two Days of Consulting
The specific nature of Aberdeen’s two full-day consulting sessions may be outlined in any manner the client deems appropriate.

Quarterly Briefings
An Aberdeen analyst takes part in quarterly interactive one-hour sessions that review recent market developments, identify what to expect from the upcoming quarter, and provide recommendations on market strategy and messages.

Strategic Inquiry Service 
Aberdeen’s Retainer Services include unlimited client interaction via telephone or e-mail with one or more Aberdeen analysts.  Strategic inquiry is designed to address the need for urgent review industry trends and issues as well analyze the latest breaking news.  

Practice Summary 
Invaluable documentation that includes high-level market analysis and encapsulates the most important market issues and trends.  Practice Summaries are the perfect tool to get those that are new to the industry quickly up to speed.

Major Research Reports 
Many of Aberdeen Group’s Practice Centers write at least one major research report within a twelve month period.  As a client of a Practice Center’s Retainer Service, you have access to any major research report produced by Practice Centers in the services that you retain.

Viewpoints 
Aberdeen Viewpoints present Aberdeen analysts’ opinions on the market and business dynamics of current industry issues. As a client of a Practice Center’s Retainer Service, you have access to all Viewpoints produced by Practice Centers in the services that you retain.

Profiles 
Aberdeen Profiles present objective analyses of suppliers and their products and strategies. As a client of a Practice Center’s Retainer Service, you have access to all Profiles produced by Practice Centers in the services that you retain.

The annual fee for each Strategic Analysis Retainer Service is $15,000 and includes provisions for two client interfaces.
A Tailored Response to Computer Associates Unique Needs
In on-going discussions, you requested tailored response to meet the unique requirements at Computer Associates.  We agreed to the following.

In our discussions Computer Associates requested that Peter Kastner prioritize the services that he thought would be of most value to Computer Associates.  The five services Peter suggested are:

Application Development and Strategic Data Management
Systems and Network Management
Information Security
Internet Infrastructure
Professional Services

Strategic, highly consultative analysis is a key differentiating factor of Aberdeen Group’s Retainer services.  In addition to maintaining a very low client to analyst ratio, each of our Retainer Services clients has a senior analyst assigned by practice area to manage the relationship and tailor service activities according to the client’s individual needs including written responses to, or assessments of client interactions.  (See Attached Aberdeen Biographies for the analysts assigned to the five services listed above.)  

Peter Kastner will continue to manage the overall consulting relationship with Computer Associates with each of the analysts directing activities within their assigned areas.

Reduction in Number of Consulting Days
A key issue that you raised in regard to our standard Retainer Service configuration is that Consulting Days and multiple Quarterly Briefings are difficult to absorb at Computer Associates.  To address your requirement, we have reconfigured the five services you selected.  Instead of two consulting days and four quarterly briefings per service, we propose one consulting day and semi-annual (twice a year) briefings.  

The special fee per service is $9,000.  The new annual fee for five services is $45,000 with invoices of $11,250 per quarter (4 * $11,250 = $45,000).

The fees quoted here are based on a minimum of five services.  The proposal is valid until December 21, 1998.  If additional consulting days are required, Computer Associates can commission additional days as needed on a project per project basis .

Intranet Access Option
We have included the right for C/A employees to access the research from these five services via the Internet.  

Research:
During the course of the contract each of the five services will write either an Impact or a Profile covering a Computer Associates product, service or strategy.  

License for Posting on the World-Wide-Web and Paper Reprints
As part of this proposal we are including special rates for purchasing Internet License for posting for external viewing and paper reprint orders (See Attachment).  

Acceptance
Aberdeen Group will accept a signed copy of this letter as a contractual document authorizing the five services.  Alternatively, we will accept a verbal purchase order authorizing us to proceed, followed by your written purchase order. 

Thank you again for the opportunity to offer our services to Computer Associates.  Please call me at (617) 854-5231 with any questions.

Sincerely,



Fred H. Abbott
Senior Vice President
