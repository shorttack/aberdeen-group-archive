### Abstract

'PCs, NCs, and NetPCs — Where Do We Go From Here?' presentation by Peter S. Kastner to the Computer Associates Acapulco CIO Conference in May 1997. Frames the late-1990s desktop dilemma between Microsoft/Intel (Wintel PC), Oracle/Sun (Network Computer / NC), and the Microsoft/Intel NetPC compromise. Analyzes desktop business drivers (intranets, extranets, JIT supply chain, globalization) and argues where each client architecture fits best.
