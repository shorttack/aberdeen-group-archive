## Abstract

Two-day Customer Focused Selling sales-training curriculum for Computer Associates, developed by Aberdeen Group. Day 1 covers behavior styles (DiSC), credibility/trust, communication, questioning technique, and customer-focused selling; Day 2 covers value proposition, price-vs-value, closing, and roleplay. The document is the structured course outline with time allocations per topic. Reflects Aberdeen's sales-force-education practice during the CA retainer era.
