Aberdeen Group, Inc.
Mutual Confidentiality Agreement

MUTUAL CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT, dated as of ________ __, 20___, by and between Aberdeen Group, Inc. and [____________].

WHEREAS, each of the parties hereto is willing to disclose certain information ("Confidential Information") regarding such party's business, financial condition, operations, assets and liabilities to the other party for purposes of evaluating certain potential business relations between them (the "Transaction"); and
WHEREAS, each of the parties hereto desires that the Confidential Information disclosed by such party be treated as proprietary and confidential information in accordance with the terms hereof.

1. Confidentiality.
(a) Each party: (i) acknowledges all Confidential Information of the other party shall be deemed confidential and proprietary; (ii) agrees to use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of evaluating the Transaction; (iii) agrees not to reproduce the Confidential Information except as necessary for such evaluation. Either party may disclose to representatives who need to know. Each party is responsible for breach by representatives.

(b) "Confidential Information" means information proprietary to the disclosing party or designated as "Confidential" including: documentation, discoveries, ideas, concepts, software, designs, drawings, specifications, techniques, models, methodologies, information, diagrams, flow charts, procedures, and know-how.

2. Exceptions.
(a) CI shall not include information which: (i) was or becomes generally available to the public other than as a result of disclosure by receiving party; (ii) available from non-confidential source; (iii) was lawfully in receiving party's possession prior to disclosure.
(b) In the event receiving party is required to disclose CI by legal process, it shall provide prompt notice so disclosing party may seek protective order.

3. Return of Confidential Information. Upon request: return all CI; no copies retained; destroy documents based on CI and certify in writing.

4. Representations and Warranties. Disclosing party excludes all warranties regarding Confidential Information.

5. Non-Disclosure of Discussions. Neither party will disclose the fact that CI has been shared, or that discussions are taking place, without prior written consent.

6. No Implied Rights. No rights of any kind in Confidential Information implied by this Agreement.

7. Nonsolicitation of Employees. For 24 months following date of this Agreement, neither party shall directly or indirectly employ, retain or solicit any employee of the other party without prior written consent.

8. Equitable Relief. Money damages would not be a sufficient remedy for breach; either party entitled to specific performance and injunctive relief; no bond requirement.

9. Governing Law. Commonwealth of Massachusetts internal laws; without giving effect to conflicts of laws principles.

10. Counterparts. May be executed in one or more counterparts.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have executed this Agreement as of the date set forth above.
