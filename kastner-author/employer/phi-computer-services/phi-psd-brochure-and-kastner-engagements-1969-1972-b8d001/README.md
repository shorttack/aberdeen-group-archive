## Abstract

Combines a primary-source PHI Update brochure (~1971-1972) describing PHI's Professional Services Division (PSD) — including Kastner's profile as a Systems Analyst in the Financial Systems Group — with Kastner's personal memoir of his March 1969 entry into PHI as a junior programmer at $7,200/year, the 1960s-era apprenticeship learning model, and his work on Marine Midland bond-portfolio Y2K-precursor changes, the First Chicago correspondent-bank DDA system, the Cumberland Farms gas-station accounting system, and the optimization of PHI's internal accounting system.
