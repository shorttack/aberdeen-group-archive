ABSTRACT:
Full-page Stratus XA2000-family launch advertisement reproduced from the ABA Banking Journal. Headline: 'ONCE AGAIN, STRATUS CATCHES THE COMPETITION WITH THEIR COMPUTERS DOWN.' Photograph shows five competitor-executives in despair. Ad introduces the **Stratus XA2000 family** (Models 110, 120, 130, 140) as the most powerful fault-tolerant computers in the world, with Model 140 executing 50+ transactions/second — three times the processing power of the previous Stratus XA600. All XA2000 systems backward-compatible with older Stratus systems. Body copy emphasizes open-ended architecture for future scaling, customer-loyalty stat ("100% of customers surveyed would not even consider changing computer companies"), modular online upgrades, and 21st-century time horizon. Footer: **'For complete information, contact your local Stratus sales office, or call Peter Kastner at (617) 460-2192.'** Address: Stratus Computer, 55 Fairbanks Boulevard, Marlboro, MA 01752.
