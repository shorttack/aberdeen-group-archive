## Abstract

Short business letter from Peter Kastner to Peter Blackmore at Compaq, written in the immediate aftermath of the September 11, 2001 attacks and the HP-Compaq merger announcement. Follows up on a May 2001 Cambridge meeting on customer communications; transmits seven Aberdeen position papers on the HP-Compaq merger integration and proposes field-sales preparation and education program work for Aberdeen. Captures tone and relationship-management style of the Aberdeen era. Aberdeen-internal employer record.
