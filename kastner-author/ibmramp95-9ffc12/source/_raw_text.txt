As a result of our recent discussions and revisions, Aberdeen Group is pleased to submit this second proposal to IBM. We propose to conduct a market research study of IBM's commercial-application RS/6000 Scalable Parallel (SP) platform installed base with the dual objectives of:
- Preparing an Aberdeen Group White Paper for the external use of the IBM sales force and customers on SP commercial customer applications and uses;
- Identifying areas and making recommendations for improved communications, expectations, and satisfaction levels of the commercial SP installed base.

IBM SP Rapid Accurate Market Positioning (RAMP)
Successful suppliers of computer and communications products and services are being obliged to make more and faster decisions in the face of new and revolutionary market dynamics. When large investments are at risk, when strategic decisions are vital for business success, and when available market information makes executives feel ill at ease, you need a fast, reliable, industry-knowledgeable source for insightful, accurate, business-oriented market analysis.

Aberdeen Group has developed a unique methodology termed RAMP for Rapid Accurate Market Positioning. Using the proven RAMP methodology, we consult with you to identify all the decision making objectives for your market-information requirements, locate and work with key IS decision makers at their sites to determine their true, not merely stated, buying intentions. Aberdeen consultants analyze this primary data to provide you with unique management-oriented findings and recommendations within sixty days.

Aberdeen's clients tell us that the results obtained from a RAMP project are more accurate and useful than those typically obtained from a series of focus groups costing twice as much and taking twice as long.

IBM SP RAMP Project Deliverables
To start the RAMP process, IBM and Aberdeen will jointly establish the detailed market-information and business-requirement objectives for your custom project. Our primary market research source will result from conducting 11 in-person interviews with SP customers:
- IBM will designate 5 North American commercial SP customers for the primary purpose of gathering site background information for success stories in a White Paper for external use by IBM in marketing the SP worldwide;
- Aberdeen will contact 6 additional North American SP customers for the primary purpose of gathering qualitative customer satisfaction information, with emphasis on how and whether the SP is meeting the customer's expectations.

Upon the completion of the RAMP process, Aberdeen will provide:
- A 15-20 page Aberdeen Group white paper on SP customer success stories. Aberdeen will supply 5,000 copies. IBM may purchase additional reprint copies: quantity 1,000 at $6.00 each; quantity 5,000 at $4.75 each. IBM may at its own expense translate the White Paper into foreign languages.
- A final written report (typically 100+ pages) with findings, conclusions, and recommendations for improving IBM's marketing to SP customers by better setting customer expectations.
