## Abstract

Executed IT@Aberdeen Gold-level Client Service Agreement between Aberdeen Group and Intel Corporation, covering 12 months from Sept 30, 2003 to Sept 29, 2004. Service fee $25,000 plus 5-unit Credit Bank ($5,000) = $30,000 total. Research topic: Client Side Computing. Peter Kastner, EVP designated as Intel's primary analyst. Intel contact: Mike Green, Hillsboro OR; Aberdeen Sales Representative: Sandra Tubbs. Deliverables include quarterly briefing, press quotes/inquiries, VC inquiries, Snapshot, one Logo in IT Newsletter, access to publications library, survey data, IT Access community, and IT research symposiums.
