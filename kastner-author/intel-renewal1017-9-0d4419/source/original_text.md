 Service Offerings
Aberdeen Research and Market Intelligence
Aberdeen Research publications are accessible to IT@Aberdeen clients via the secured, members’ only section of the Aberdeen Web site.  Using a unique user name and password, IT@Aberdeen members will be able to access the following research and market intelligence:
Primary Research Data and Analysis: IT@Aberdeen members will have access to the raw data of IT research published projects.  This raw data is typically presented in a downloadable Excel spreadsheet that can be manipulated by the client.  Aberdeen will also perform cross-tabulations and analyses at the client’s request. 
Market Research Reports: IT@Aberdeen members will have access to any new Aberdeen market research report in their Research Topic area(s) and may receive additional Research Reports in related topic areas, as Aberdeen deems appropriate. 
Aberdeen White Papers, Viewpoints, Profiles and Impacts: IT@Aberdeen members will also have access to all new Aberdeen research and news analysis documents published in all IT Related Topic areas. 
Aberdeen Insights, Perspectives, SnapShots, and Solution Spotlights:  These Web-only Thought Leadership and research documents are available on the secured Aberdeen Web site. 
Online Research Publication Library:  All Aberdeen research documents, thought leadership publications and survey research are available to IT@Aberdeen clients on the secured Aberdeen Web site. 
Marketing Consulting and Advisory Services
IT@Aberdeen clients can access members of the IT research team in the following ways:
Ad hoc telephone inquiry:  Reasonable telephone access to the Aberdeen analyst designated as your account manager and, through him/her, access to other members of the IT Research team.  The analyst account manager can also arrange for limited access to analysts in other Aberdeen Research Practice Areas, including Enterprise and Internet Infrastructure, Data Knowledge and Analytics, and Services.  Phone access is generally used for querying analysts about specific market events, vendors and products, and market trends.
Advisory Services and Consulting:  Competitive intelligence, market positioning, and marketing communications and strategy consulting services are available at set intervals for IT@Aberdeen members.  Clients typically use these services for gaining intelligence on competitive offerings, conducting product gap analysis, identifying and executing due diligence on merger and partner candidates, determining marketing and positioning strategies, including “beta testing” press and analyst relations materials, critiquing sales collateral, review of business plans, and determination of enterprise functional business requirements. 
Account Management: An Aberdeen analyst will be designated to manage each client.  Subscribers will be matched to the analyst with the best correlation of analyst and supplier focus, and this analyst will serve as the primary point of contact for IT@Aberdeen Advisory Services issues.
Peter Kastner, EVP is designated as Intel’s primary analyst.
