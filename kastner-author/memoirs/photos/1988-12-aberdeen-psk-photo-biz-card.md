# Peter S. Kastner — Aberdeen Group business card photograph (December 1988)

**Type:** memoir / photo-metadata (skill §13.3 lightweight treatment)
**Date:** December 1988

**Role at time of photo:** Vice President (newly appointed to lead Aberdeen Transaction Services)
**Source file:** `Aberdeen-psk-1988-12-photo-biz-card-6.pdf`

## Description

A black-and-white portrait photograph of Peter S. Kastner paired with Aberdeen
Group business-card contact information. Produced in December 1988 as part of
Aberdeen's corporate press-kit materials announcing Kastner's appointment as
Vice President and head of the new Aberdeen Transaction Services business unit
(see companion file `Aberdeen-beat-sheet-1988-12-psk-pr-5.pdf`).

The composition combines a head-and-shoulders studio portrait with the Aberdeen
Group wordmark and the firm's 12 Marshall Street, Boston address block.
