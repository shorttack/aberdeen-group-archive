## Description

A multi-page PDF compilation of **title slides and agenda openers** from six
distinct Peter S. Kastner speaking engagements during the 1994–1996 period.
Each page captures the visual opener of a specific Aberdeen-Group keynote
or training delivery, useful as a primary-source index to Kastner's mid-1990s
speaking circuit. The full talks themselves are not included — only the
title slides, with venue/date/topic identification.
