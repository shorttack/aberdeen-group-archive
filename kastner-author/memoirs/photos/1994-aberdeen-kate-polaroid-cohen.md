## Description

A Polaroid-style photograph of **Peter S. Kastner** with a woman identified
in the file's name as **"Kate"**, decorated in mixed-media style with
hand-painted embellishments by Boston-area artist **Barbara E. Cohen**:

- A whimsical white "hat" painted onto Kastner's head
- Cartoon-style oversized glasses painted onto Kate's face

The use of a Polaroid base, the bright-color overpainting, and the signed
copyright mark are characteristic of Cohen's mixed-media practice.

The artifact appears to have been a personalized gift — most likely an
Aberdeen-Group office memento or a friendship/occasion piece — created during
Kastner's Aberdeen tenure.
