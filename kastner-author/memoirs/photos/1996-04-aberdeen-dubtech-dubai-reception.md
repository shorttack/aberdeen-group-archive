## Description

A photograph and short caption from the *Khaleej Times* of Dubai under the
headline **"Boston-Based Firm at Dubai Show"** documenting an Aberdeen Group
reception held at the Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza during DubTech '96. The caption
reads:

> *"The Aberdeen Group from Boston, which is in Dubai to take part at DubTech
> '96, was given a reception at Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza. Sultan bin Sulayem,
> chairman and managing director of Dubai Ports Authority and Jebel Ali Free
> Zone Authority, who was the chief guest, is seen with Peter Kastner, John
> Logan, James Hurley and Ali Kamali, at the reception. — KT photo"*

## Pictured (persons named)

- **Sultan Ahmed bin Sulayem** — chief guest; chairman and managing director,
  Dubai Ports Authority and Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority (later founder/chairman
  of DP World).
- **Peter S. Kastner** — Aberdeen Group co-founder and senior analyst.
- **John R. Logan** — Aberdeen Group Executive Vice President.
- **James Hurley** — Aberdeen Group analyst (mid-1990s era).
- **Ali Kamali** — local host / Dubai event partner (regional context).

## Event context

DubTech '96 was a Dubai information-technology trade show. Aberdeen Group
travelled from Boston as a participating analyst firm and hosted a client/VIP
reception at the Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza. The presence of Sultan bin Sulayem
as chief guest indicates the reception's positioning at the intersection of
Aberdeen's IT-analyst credibility and Dubai's emerging free-zone /
ports-technology agenda — a precursor to what would become the Dubai Internet
City and DP World era.
