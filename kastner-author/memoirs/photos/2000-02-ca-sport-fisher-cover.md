## Description

A high-resolution scan of a mock-up magazine cover titled **"CA Sport Fisher"**
designed in the style of a sport-fishing trade magazine. The cover features
Peter S. Kastner seated in a fighting-chair on a sportfishing boat, wearing
a baseball cap and casual gear. Cover blurbs reference an "Atlantis: Hotspot"
issue and a vessel named "UTICA." The masthead identifies the piece as
"Vol I — Issue 1 — Feb 19 2000" with "Computer Associates publication"
attribution.

This is a one-off keepsake created by (or commissioned by) Computer Associates
International, Inc. (CA) — almost certainly produced as a thank-you or
event memento for Kastner's role as a CA-affiliated industry analyst during
the late-1990s / early-2000s era.
