## Description

`PSK-10.tiff` is a 12-frame multi-page TIFF that bundles a mixed set of
personal-archive items spanning multiple decades of Kastner family and career
history. JPEG previews of each frame are stored in this directory under
`PSK-10-previews/PSK-10-{0..11}.jpg`. The original 207 MB multi-frame TIFF is
retained outside the repository (in Kastner's personal archive and the
working session's OneDrive backup), since it exceeds GitHub's 100 MB
per-file size limit.

The frames are individually itemized below and constitute several distinct
artifact-classes; this single memoir indexes them rather than fragmenting
into 12 separate files. Specific frames may be promoted into their own
memoir files later if higher-fidelity context becomes available.
