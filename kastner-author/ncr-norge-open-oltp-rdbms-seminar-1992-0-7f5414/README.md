## Abstract

Mini-seminar 'Challenges and opportunities with transaction processing in an open environment' (Norwegian: 'Utfordringer og muligheter med transaksjonsprosessering i et åpent miljø') hosted by NCR Norge AS in Oslo on 11 September 1992. Three lecturers: (1) Peter S. Kastner, Vice President, Commercial Systems Practice, Aberdeen Group, on 'What a manager needs to know about transaction processing in an open environment'; (2) Sven Aamodt, Technical Director, Oracle Norge AS, on synergy effects between TP and relational databases; (3) Scott Hansen, Managing Director, Unix International European division, on UNIX System V transaction processing under the Unix International ATLAS architecture / Corporate Hub Environment. Kastner's biography lists his 1990 Aberdeen reports 'Transaction Processing into the 1990s' and 'The Hardware Suppliers', his 1991 'Evaluating Options in Microprocessor Multiprocessing' report, plus prior lecturing for Apple, ASK Ingres, DEC, Hewlett-Packard, NCR, and Stratus Computer, and previous head-of-marketing/sales roles at DEC, Prime, and Stratus, plus Arthur D. Little consulting. Two source files reconciled: the Norwegian PDF (#5, with original Norwegian Bokmål agenda but English-language abstracts because the speaker abstracts were already in English in the source) and the clean English DOCX (#7) which is used as primary text. A tiny 82-byte test fragment (#6) containing only the Norwegian title is noted but adds no content. Includes the participant list (~30 Norwegian attendees from DnB, Fokus Bank, Fellesdata, banks, banks user-group, government bodies, and IDC Norge) and the NCR participants list (11 NCR Norge staff including host Steinar Høistad). Attachments referenced are NCR TopEND brochure, NCR Model 3550 brochure (UNIX V.4 multiprocessor up to eight i486-50MHz, 320 MIPS, 1,000+ workstations), and 'The World of NCR' overview. Documents Kastner's international (Norway) delivery of the same Open OLTP thesis the Korean translation (Batch 23 study aberdeen-open-oltp-enterprise-managers-korean) and the English fragments (Batch 22 ea272f) cover.
