## Abstract

Oracle Corporation seminar tour brochure for 'Transitioning to Customer-Driven Information Solutions', sponsored by Oracle and co-presented with AT&T/NCR, running fall 1993 across eight US cities: Pittsburgh (Tue Oct 12), Dallas (Thu Oct 14), Los Angeles (Tue Oct 19), Seattle (Thu Oct 21), Cincinnati (Thu Oct 28), Atlanta (Tue Nov 2), Boston (Thu Nov 4), and New York (Tue Nov 9). Peter S. Kastner, Vice President, Aberdeen Group, was the keynote speaker, with the agenda flowing 8:30 registration / 9:00 introduction / 9:10 Kastner keynote / 10:00 break / 10:15 AT&T-NCR and Oracle delivering Customer-Driven Information Solutions / 11:15 AT&T-NCR + Oracle case studies / 11:45 Q&A / 12:00 noon close. Brochure features Kastner biography verifying his Aberdeen role, Corporate Computing practice responsibility, industry-cluster experience (manufacturing, banking, financial services, healthcare, government), and identifies Aberdeen Group as 'Boston-based computer-systems, software, and communications consulting and research organization'. Reservation hotline: 1-800-442-8649 ext. 24. Brochure code WAA1XX. Sister artifact to the 1992 NCR Norge OLTP seminar (Batch 23) and the 1993 Land O'Lakes IS-strategy seminar (Batch 22) — together documents Kastner's mid-career role as a roving keynote anchor for AT&T-NCR + Oracle channel marketing.
