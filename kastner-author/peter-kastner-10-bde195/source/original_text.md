04:05 PM-04:15 PM	Break
04:15 PM-05:00 PM	Intel Experts Panel

Lab Tour Descriptions: 
    • Microprocessor Validation Lab Tour:  Behind the scenes look at the CPU post-silicon validation lab and various test methods, platforms, and debug tools used
    • vPro Technology Business Client & Enterprise Platform Integration Lab Tour: vPro at work: An up close look at the working labs driving vPro(tm) technology from concept to end user adoption
    • DataCenter Tour: See one of Intel's newest lights-out data centers located on the Jones Farm Campus.  This "lights out" data center, built as part of Intel's recent datacenter consolidation effort program, includes two 6000 square foot modules each operating at over 500 watts per square foot.
 Breakout Descriptions:
    • Notebook Strategies & Plans (JFCC 121&122):  This is an opportunity to get an overview from Karen Regis and Uday Marty, Mobile Products Group Marketing Managers, of Intel’s strategies and plans for ’09 and a sneak peak in 2010 which includes a next generation platform overview and roadmap update
•       Larabee Programmability (JFCC 119):  Broad overview of the Larrabee architecture and the new vector instruction-set used to program it, the Larrabee new instructions (LRBni)
•       Mobile Internet Platforms (JFCC 120):    This breakout with Erik Reid, Dir of Mobile Products Group Marketing, and Gary Willihnganz, Dir of Ultra Mobile Group Marketing, will utilize a roundtable format to enable discussion on topics regarding Intel’s MID, netbooks and nettop efforts, strategy and product plans.  Come ready to chat!
