## Abstract

A formal 16 Personality Factors (16PF) psychometric assessment conducted on Peter S. Kastner dated 17 July 2001. The report evaluates Kastner across four domains: interpersonal relationships, coping with pressure, decision making and problem solving, and team behaviour, characterizing him as an extravert, resilient coordinator-type with strong social facility, dominant assertiveness, practical rather than abstract thinking, and a preference for collaborative decision-making.
