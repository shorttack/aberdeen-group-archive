### Abstract

Slide-format webinar presentation by Aberdeen Group covering SOA adoption findings from 2005 research and the 2006 enterprise integration research agenda. Key findings: 92% of companies at or below 25% SOA adoption rate; over half predict 50%+ SOA-based software in 5 years; top SOA motives include managing IT integration costs (50%), business alignment (44%), and implementation speed (43%). Best-in-class KPIs show 29.6% innovation spend vs 18.5% average, and 12.4% maintenance costs vs 27.3% average. Outlines Q1-Q4 2006 research roadmap covering applications on the network, SOA development, edge computing, and mid-market strategies.
