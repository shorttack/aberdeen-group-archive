## Abstract

Master procurement agreement establishing the multinational procurement relationship between Sun Microsystems (Buyer) and Aberdeen Group (Supplier) under which Aberdeen provides Deliverables and Services via SOWs. Contains full commercial terms (definitions, term/termination, pricing, warranties including Y2K and euro readiness, indemnification, IP rights tied to white-paper logo/trademark, NY/local governing law). Signed by Peter S. Kastner as Chief Operating Officer of Aberdeen Group — biographical confirmation of his COO role at Aberdeen in August 2001 (compare to 'EVP Research' title on Sun questionnaire Study 9, June 2002).
