## Abstract

Written October 5, 2001 -- less than a month after 9/11 -- this letter from Peter Kastner to IT supplier executives presents Aberdeen Group's analysis that Q4 2001 technology spending will decrease likely by double digits, followed by markedly lower growth in 2002 compared to historic norms. The letter describes how Aberdeen is adapting to the post-9/11 environment through five-tier conferencing technology and e-learning, and urges IT suppliers to think out of the box to emerge with market-share gains from the IT recession of 2001-2002.
