## Overview

Aberdeen Group's comprehensive profile of Cabletron Systems assessing its technical leadership, strategic challenges, and market positioning. The title metaphor ("beacon under the basket") captures Aberdeen's thesis: Cabletron had outstanding technology that was systematically underappreciated due to limited marketing investments and narrow Fortune 500 channel focus. Aberdeen documents Cabletron's SecureFast VLAN architecture, Spectrum network management platform, SmartSwitch product family, CyberSWITCH remote access line, and ambitious World/One global expansion program.

A key analytical contribution is Aberdeen's concept of the Public-Private Switched Network (PPSN) — a predicted market where CPE and carrier equipment would converge. Aberdeen ranked Cabletron as a top-tier PPSN competitor. In reality, Cabletron's revenue peaked in 1997 and the company was reorganized and broken up by 2000.
