# Commercial RISC/Unix 1996: HP Achieves Over 50% Share In 28% Growth Market

**Study ID:** 1997-commercial-risc-unix-1996-mvp-711569  
**Publisher:** AberdeenGroup  
**Date:** February 3, 1997  
**Type:** Market Viewpoint (MVP)  
**Series:** Volume 10 / Number 1  

## Overview

Aberdeen's seventh annual survey of the commercial, multiuser RISC/Unix marketplace. The 1996 worldwide commercial multiuser RISC/Unix production server market grew 28% to $18.2 billion. Hewlett-Packard achieved 52% market share, IBM held 18%, and Sun Microsystems showed the fastest growth at 73%. The report projects continued strong growth and examines the Intel IA-64 transition.
