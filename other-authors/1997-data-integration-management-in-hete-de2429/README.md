**Subject:** Evolutionary Technologies International (ETI) — ETI•EXTRACT Tool Suite  

## Overview

Aberdeen Group profile of Evolutionary Technologies International (ETI) and its ETI•EXTRACT Tool Suite for data integration management in heterogeneous computing environments. The study covers ETI's approach to data warehousing, Year 2000 conversion, client/server migration, and heterogeneous platform integration. ETI had $25.5M in FY1997 revenues with 50-100% annual growth and Fortune 1000 customers including Procter & Gamble, Hallmark, and PHH.
