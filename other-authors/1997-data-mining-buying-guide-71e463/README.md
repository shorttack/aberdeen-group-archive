**Type:** Market Research Report (Buying Guide)  
**Authors:** David Hill & Robert Moran  

## Overview

Aberdeen Group's Enterprise Data Mining Buying Guide (1997 Edition) profiling eight leading commercial-grade data mining suppliers: ANGOSS International Ltd., Data Distilleries B.V., HyperParallel Inc., IBM Corp., Magnify Inc., NeoVista Software Inc., Syllogic B.V., and Thinking Machines Corp. Evaluates vendors using a five-dimension framework: vertical focus, platforms, algorithms, qualifiers (lifecycle support, architecture, ease of use, professional services, company strength), and pricing. Priced at $495 for the 97-page full report.
