## Abstract

Aberdeen Group profiles Insession, Inc., a middleware software vendor whose solution enables integration of heterogeneous distributed environments with existing host-based enterprise transaction systems. The brief profile argues that competitive pressures are driving IS to build new applications rapidly and cost-effectively on top of enterprise-scale distributed "accessware." Insession is positioned as enabling organizations to preserve and leverage existing ETS investments while integrating with new distributed environments including the Internet and client-server applications.

## Context

Insession, Inc. was subsequently acquired by Transaction Systems Architects, Inc. (TSA/ACI Worldwide), where it became the Insession Technologies business unit. The ICE family of products (connectivity software for HP NonStop servers) remained a revenue contributor accounting for ~13% of ACI's revenues through 2005. The ICE product was eventually renamed ICE/XS and became a component of ACI's BASE24-eps payment system.
