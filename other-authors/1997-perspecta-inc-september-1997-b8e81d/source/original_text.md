### Abstract

Aberdeen Group profiles Perspecta Inc., a San Francisco startup that developed OLAP-I (OLAP for Information) — a server-based technology applying OLAP-style analytics to non-numeric content. The SmartContent system dynamically reorganizes information in real-time based on user behavior. Aberdeen endorsed Perspecta as a strong candidate for applications requiring more effective information navigation interfaces.
