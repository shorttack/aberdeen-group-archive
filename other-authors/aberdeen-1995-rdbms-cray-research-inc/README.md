## Summary

Aberdeen Group product profile examining Cray Research's strategic pivot from scientific/government supercomputing to commercial RDBMS markets. The study evaluates the CS6400 SMP server platform against competitors IBM SP2 and AT&T GIS 3600, covers the formation of the Cray Research Business Systems Division, and recommends that IS decision makers evaluate Cray for high-end UNIX/RDBMS deployments.

Published months before Cray Research's acquisition by Silicon Graphics (SGI) for $740M in February 1996. The CS6400 product line was subsequently divested by SGI to Sun Microsystems, where it evolved into the highly successful Sun Enterprise 10000 (Starfire).
