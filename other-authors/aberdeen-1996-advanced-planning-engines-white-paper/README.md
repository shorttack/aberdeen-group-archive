# aberdeen-1996-advanced-planning-engines-white-paper

**Title:** Advanced Planning Engine Technologies: Can Capital-Generating Technology Change the Face of Manufacturing?  
**Author:** Aberdeen Group  
**Date:** February 1996  
**Type:** white-paper  
**License:** CC-BY-4.0  

## Summary

Aberdeen Group executive white paper examining Advanced Planning Engine Technologies (APET) — an early term for what became modern supply chain planning and APS software. Profiles seven key vendors with 1995 revenue data, anonymous enterprise ROI case studies (including $5.5M distribution savings, 70% order cost reduction, 150% productivity improvement), and technical evaluation criteria. Argues APET should be the first technology investment on the enterprise reengineering path.

## Assessment
