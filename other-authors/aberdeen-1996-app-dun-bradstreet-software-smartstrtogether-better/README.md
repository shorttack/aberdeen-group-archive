## Summary

Aberdeen Group product profile of D&B Software's SmartStream Distributed Enterprise (SmartStream DE) v4.0 ERP suite. Examines the distributed client/server architecture using Sybase/MS SQL Server, three-form workflow capabilities, and market competition with Oracle, PeopleSoft, SAP, and SSA. With $100M+ in SmartStream revenues and 10,000+ total customer sites, D&B Software is profiled at a high point just before its parent Dun & Bradstreet Corporation divests it.

D&B Software was subsequently acquired by Geac Computer Corporation. Geac abandoned the SmartStream manufacturing module in 1997.
