# aberdeen-1996-as400-year-2000-ibm-leading-users

**Title:** AS/400 and the Year 2000: IBM Leading Users Across an Unknown Chasm  
**Author:** Aberdeen Group  
**Date:** September 27, 1996 (Volume 9, Number 17)  
**Type:** market-viewpoint  
**License:** CC-BY-4.0  

## Summary

Aberdeen Group market viewpoint examining the Y2K problem for IBM AS/400 and System/3X installed bases. Published 3+ years before Y2K, it praises IBM's AS/400 division for proactive leadership: OS/400 v3 declared Year 2000 safe, compliant software lists published, ISV compliance statements requested, professional services recruited, and date-assessment tools developed. Contrasts IBM's approach favorably against other hardware suppliers reluctant to engage.
