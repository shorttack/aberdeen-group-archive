## Abstract

Aberdeen evaluates Bull's Sagister, a hardware/software/services package combining PowerPC RISC/UNIX with mainframe operational tooling. Covers Sagister's four technology tiers, three hardware configurations (M1/M2/M3), and competitive analysis versus IBM RS/6000 SP, HP, DEC TruCluster, and CA-Unicenter.
