## Abstract

Aberdeen evaluates Cisco ABU's 1996 product rollout and end-to-end remote access strategy. Study covers AS5200 universal access server, 750 series SOHO, CiscoRemote, CiscoSecure, CiscoPro channel program, and IOS as de facto networking standard.
