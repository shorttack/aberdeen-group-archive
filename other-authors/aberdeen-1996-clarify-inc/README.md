## Abstract

Aberdeen evaluates Clarify Inc.'s integrated best-of-breed CIS suite (ClearSupport, ClearSales, ClearHelpdesk, CommCenter), 99% uptime reliability, linchpin accounts at Microsoft/Cisco/HP/MCI/Sprint, and Metropolis acquisition. Aberdeen concludes Clarify is 'a CIS winner' — validated by Nortel's $2.1B acquisition in 1999.
