## Abstract

This Aberdeen Group profile evaluates Digital Equipment Corporation's comprehensive NT-across-the-enterprise strategy. Aberdeen concludes that Digital is uniquely positioned to become the leading enterprise NT supplier because none of its major competitors — IBM, HP, or Sun — are strategically committed to NT, leaving Digital unchallenged at the high end. The study examines four IT executive buying criteria, a full product line, ISV portfolio, systems management capabilities, and the Digital-Microsoft relationship.
