## Abstract

This Aberdeen Group profile evaluates Digital Equipment Corporation's Personal Workstation line as a low-cost Unix workstation alternative. Using Pro/Engineer, CADalyst, and Ansys benchmarks, Aberdeen demonstrates that the Alpha XL 366 MHz outperforms SGI and HP Unix workstations at approximately half the cost. The study examines Digital's indirect channel strategy through VARs and identifies five target markets.
