## Abstract

This Aberdeen Group White Paper evaluates four migration strategies for IBM System/36 owners: stay put, migrate to Advanced 36, migrate to AS/400, or migrate to Windows NT PC-LAN. Based on field interviews and a five-criteria decision framework, Aberdeen recommends the Advanced 36 as clearly the best choice. The study documents Y2K compliance urgency and provides detailed cost tables for 10- and 20-user configurations.
