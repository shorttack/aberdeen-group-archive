## Abstract

Aberdeen Group's whitepaper examines the maturation of three-tier client-server computing in 1996, presenting the "Three-Tier Plus" model that adds MPP/clustered SMP as a fourth tier for data warehousing. The study synthesizes field research showing that organizations achieve four key business outcomes: improved enterprise efficiency, multi-source information access, scalability for emerging technologies, and superior ROI. Aberdeen argues OOP is critical for partitioning applications across tiers and predicts NT Server will close the gap with Unix on mid-tier within 24 months.
