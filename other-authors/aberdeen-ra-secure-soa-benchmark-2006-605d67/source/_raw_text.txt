The Secure Service-Oriented Architecture Benchmark Report 
 
Business Value Point of View: 
 
ervice Oriented Architecture (SOA) creates business 
benefits within an existing application infrastructure. 
An SOA platform restructures the business value 
chain, which creates new pressures for secure business proc-
ess flow control, metadata management, and service man-
agement within companies and between partner organiza-
tions.  
 
This report will reveal the technologies and solutions or-
ganizations use to secure SOA. Key findings of this report 
will provide insights into:  
 
 
The secure SOA technology solutions and ap-
proaches used by organizations to facilitate change, 
shared services, and federated identity 
 
How to secure a loosely-coupled SOA architecture without tradeoffs in usability and access 
 
The importance of standards and out-of-the box integration for a secure SOA platform 
 
The connection between secure SOA and operation management and governance 
 
Research approach: The research methodology consists of a broad-based survey of enterprises 
either using or considering the purchase technologies to address risk management as well as inter-
views with end-users and IT vendors.  
Our research network reach: This study will be marketed to Aberdeen’s online community of 
Global 5000 executives and decision-makers, in addition to Aberdeen’s affiliate research channel 
partners, with a total reach in excess of one million value chain executives. 
End-user audience: Target readers include CIOs, CSOs, CROs, CTOs, corporate executives, IT 
managers, system architects, and information technology managers.
