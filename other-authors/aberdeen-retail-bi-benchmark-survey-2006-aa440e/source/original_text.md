--- Slide 2 ---
Research Hypothesis & Objectives
Hypothesis:
Advanced business intelligence, which includes predictive analytics improves top and bottom line retailer performance.  These analytics extend across the enterprise from assortment, price and promotion planning, through monitoring the sales and profit implications of actions initiated in one part of the organization on the enterprise as a whole.
Research Justification:
Business intelligence and predictive analytics have emerged as key technologies used by retailers today. 
Objectives:
QUANTIFY the value BI brings to retailers’ top line
QUANTIFY the bottom line improvements retailers achieve from early warning signals distributed across the enterprise 
QUALIFY the value of scorecarding the retail organism as a whole, rather than just measuring the individual parts.
