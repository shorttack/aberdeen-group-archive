## Abstract

Boston Globe Emerging Business listing (October 1995) by Ronald Rosenberg announcing the MIT Enterprise Forum 18 October 1995 event 'Who Cares if the Computer Breaks' led by Peter S. Kastner of Aberdeen Group, with case study by Lawrence Genovesi, president of Canton-based Powerstation Technologies Inc. Forum was at MIT 77 Massachusetts Ave., Room 10-250, 6-9pm; registration $8 members / $12 non-members. The column also carries a correction concerning OraVax Inc. (Cambridge) vs GelTex Pharmaceuticals Inc. (Waltham). Brief but concrete: confirms Kastner's mid-1990s public-speaking footprint on fault-tolerance for the MIT Enterprise Forum audience.
