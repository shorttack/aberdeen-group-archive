### Abstract

BusinessWeek's 16 November 1981 launch feature on Stratus Computer Inc. as a fail-safe-computing competitor to Tandem. Quotes Stratus president William E. Foster, IDC consultant John C. Hart, Cupertino consultant David E. Gold, ADL's Norman S. Zimbel, Tandem software-VP Dennis L. McEvoy, IVP managing partner Reid W. Dennis, and Dataquest's Richard J. Matlack. Establishes Stratus's $148K starting price vs Tandem's ~$264K, projects $5B fault-tolerant market by 1985, and notes Tandem's 142% three-year sales CAGR to $109M in 1980.
