## Abstract

Hewlett-Packard's MFASC'92 (Mainframe Alternative Solution Conference '92) deck by Gustavo Fernandez — 'Shattering the Glass House / HP: The Best Alternative to Mainframe Computing'. Pitches the technology shift (>10x performance growth, 16-way MP), top IS issues (alignment, BPR, information architecture, data utilization), and detailed five-year cost-justification scenarios comparing IBM-mainframe maintain vs HP-migration paths. Cites Forrester Research and 'The P.A. Report' as third-party validation. Vendor parallel/foil to Aberdeen's 1992-1993 mainframe-alternative thesis.
