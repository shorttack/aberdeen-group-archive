                               The Steps ..
                     Moving from the Mainframe to HP
           Step 6. Assess the technology change
           - Whether centralized or distributed, have you determined the
             rightsize system(s)?
           - DASD space requirements (HP systems lend to use 30% less DASO or disk than
             mainframes for the same amount of data)?
           - Can you move away from heavy dependence on tape processing (HP systems support
             devices like 348073490s: however, rather than used as a production device. HP
             systems typically use tape as a backup or data transfer media)?
           - What happens 10 3270 devices (PCs in many cases have better economies than
             3270s due to their high maintenance cost)?
           - How many terminal/chent devices are neeoed (HP systems are highly interactive
             and the number of users tends to go up)?
           - How are existing print jobs going to be supported (HP provides printing based
             on industry standard PCL from the desktop to the data center: additionally.
             firms like iDATA provide conversion tools from IBM's AFP and OGL to PCL)?
