## Abstract

CloudAve blog post (May 19 2009, Zoli Erdos) criticizing Sprint/Palm's pricing of the upcoming Palm Pre smartphone: advertised at $199 with qualifying data plan but requiring a $100 mail-in rebate, so effective immediate price $299. Erdos cites BusinessWeek's 2005 'Great Rebate Runaround' report by Brian Grow, where 'Peter S. Kastner, a director of consulting firm Vericours Inc.' estimated 'fully 40% of all rebates never get redeemed,' translating to $2B+ extra revenue annually for retailers and suppliers. Erdos adds a personal Handspring Treo rebate horror story and argues for '$299 transparency' over deceptive rebate structures. This is syndication of a 2005 BusinessWeek Kastner/Vericours quote, wrapped in 2009 Palm Pre commentary by Erdos.
