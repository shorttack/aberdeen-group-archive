### Abstract

Short Kastner guest column from InternetWeek (January 7, 2003), mirrored on Techno-News Blog. Kastner observes that the prior corporate-standard 2.0-GHz Pentium 4 has been displaced in just three months: Dell, HP, and IBM all added the 2.4-GHz Pentium 4 to the single-price lower Pentium 4 tier, so corporate IT managers ordering a 1.8-, 2.0-, 2.2-, or 2.4-GHz desktop all pay the same price at checkout. Short-form memoir-style commentary on the 2002-2003 CPU clock-speed race.
