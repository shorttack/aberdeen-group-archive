## Abstract

Rebate & fulfillment industry trade-press article on santella.com/JPS citing Aberdeen Group's Peter Kastner: about 60 percent of buyers who could redeem computer-related rebates do not try, and of the 40 percent who try, half experience problems. The piece contextualizes the stat with a process-level explanation of why rebate redemption fails: three-party (manufacturer/retailer/fulfillment-house) coordination, 15-90 day sponsor-to-fulfillment-house payment cycles, check-clearance waits, and unpaid invoices that fulfillment houses have reported to the FTC. This is a distinct publication from the Gary Gray (Chatham Journal) and Lorrie Grant (USA Today) rebate articles in the same collection — it pairs Kastner's stat with rebate-industry supply-side process detail.
