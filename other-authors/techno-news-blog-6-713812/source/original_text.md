### Abstract

Ray Schroeder's Techno-News Blog (Jan 7 2003) reblog of Peter Kastner's InternetWeek PC buying-guide guest column titled 'Take Her Up to 2.4 GHz, Mr. Scott.' Kastner, writing in his InternetWeek column voice, argues that the 2.0-GHz Pentium 4 has been the corporate-mainstream standard for the past three months but is now being displaced: Dell, HP, and IBM have added 2.4-GHz Pentium 4 to their single-price lower-Pentium-4 price tier, meaning corporate IT managers can order a desktop with a 1.8-, 2.0-, 2.2-, or 2.4-GHz Pentium 4 for the exact same price at checkout. Classic Kastner practitioner-focused PC-procurement advice: take the faster chip, it's free. The Techno-News Blog is the preservation channel — Ray Schroeder (University of Illinois at Springfield OTEL editor) reblogged the Kastner column with attribution and source reference.
