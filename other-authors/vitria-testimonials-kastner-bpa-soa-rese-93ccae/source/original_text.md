 Customers, Partners and Analysts all agree -- Vitria customers gain a competitive edge with our solutions 

 Here are just a few of the comments that our customers, partners and the media have made about Vitria. 

"Vitria's BPA vision aligns with our recent primary research: getting to process excellence by traditional means requires complex, custom, and costly integration of applications; users want better and more timely views into their business processes; and, buyers believe an SOA stack is the right integration strategy for the future."

Peter S. Kastner, Vice President of Enterprise Integration Research, AberdeenGroup, Inc. 

"Goodyear placed a particular emphasis on the ability to execute on our business-to-business initiatives. With Vitria, we are able to automate purchase order processing across 50 systems in 32 countries and to give our distributors a real-time view of inventory."

Thomas Henderson, Director of e-Commerce Integrated Services, The Goodyear Tire & 
Rubber Company

"Vitria completely understands the roadblocks we face in our business and has developed these Business Process Applications with companies like us in mind. With Vitria.s technology, we expect to not only significantly reduce our implementation costs and risk, but provide better service to our customers and partners."

Rich Baughman, Senior Director, Integration, SureWest Communications 

"BP's long term driver for integration is the creation of global process efficiency across the business, in order to reduce costs and improve profitability. The integration work completed with Vitria contributes to this long term objective and we are committed to making Vitria our worldwide process integration standard."

John Melo, Downstream Vice President for Digital Business, BP 

"Vitria Professional Services offered the right blend of expert advisory services and implementation skills, backed by state-of-the-art tools, best practices and methodologies, enabling us to deliver a very robust application on schedule and on budget."

Dave Mewes, Chief Information Officer, MasterBrand Cabinets, Inc. 

"Vitria has helped ALLTEL integrate customer sales, service, and provisioning systems to increase ALLTEL's ability to provide customers with the communication tools and services they demand."

Brian Highfield, EAI Program Manager, ALLTEL Communications, Inc. 

"To be competitive, today's business leaders must integrate their applications and automate mission-critical business processes across their own systems and those of their partners and customers. Frank Danza, Managing Director, BearingPoint

"Vitria Professional Services is a true partner on our journey to IT excellence. Their collaborative approach, as well as the world class capabilities they bring to the table, is indispensable to any organization that wants to stay ahead of the curve."

Janet McQueen, Chief Information Officer, Blue Care Network of Michigan 

"Resolution Accelerator allows customers to sharply reduce or eliminate exceptions and automate and accelerate resolutions.
